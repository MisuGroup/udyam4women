Our Mission

Udyam Women Empowerment Foundation works towards uplifting and empowering women through skill development, educational programs, and community support initiatives. We strive to create sustainable opportunities and a nurturing environment for women across all sections of society.

Our Products & Services

We support women entrepreneurs by promoting their handcrafted products, organizing workshops, and providing market access. Our services also include capacity-building programs, financial literacy, and networking opportunities to help women succeed.
